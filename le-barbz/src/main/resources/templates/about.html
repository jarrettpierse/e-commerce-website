The Barbz Story

At Le Barbz we come to work every day because we want to solve the biggest problem in the world right now. How do save money, spread a meaningful message and look good all at once? This is a question that has fascinated us for nearly two decades. After years of research, we have discovered the secret formula to solve this problem.

Le Barbz are here to inform, entertain, inspire and clothe you. On our website you will find a range of items to suit your needs. Our products are perfect gifts for birthdays, weddings, aniversaries, graduations, christmas, valentines day, St. Patrick's day, Halloween, St. Brigid's Day, National Pizza Day, and every other day of the year. We carry a wide range of aparrel as well as cutlery and fashion face masks. We pride ourselves in the quality of out being super excellent and made from 100% recycled materials.
