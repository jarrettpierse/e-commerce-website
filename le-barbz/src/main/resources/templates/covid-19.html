Covid-19 Guidance

As many facets of life have been altered by the novel coronavirus (COVID-19), it is important to us that we ensure your customer experience does not change. To help keep our customers and colleagues safe, Le Barbz has closed all our physical store locations. We have also suspended all workshops and face-to-face meetings, and will continue to evaluate this rapidly evolving situation. The health and safety of our staff, customers, and community is our top priority.

We have introduced contact-free delivery for all our orders. You do not need to take any action to enable this. Our couriers are offering this as a standard. You may experience some delays to your order's delivery and currently we are unable to offer our next-day delivery service.

Stores in the Republic of Ireland are expected to reopen in late April. We want to ensure that our stores remain a safe place and so we ask that you continue to follow all social distancing guidelines given by the government. We are currently installing additional safety measures such as perspex screens, sanitation stations and spaced aisles.
